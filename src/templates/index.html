Best Dishes Of Zaytuna

Mandi

A fragrant dish featuring rice cooked with tender meat and a unique blend of spices, resulting in a savory and aromatic meal.

Shawarma

Grilled, marinated meat sliced thinly, often wrapped in pita bread with fresh vegetables and a savory garlic sauce.

Kabsa

A hearty rice dish that incorporates well-seasoned meat, saffron, and a mix of spices, creating a colorful and flavorful plate.
